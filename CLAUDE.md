## What is this project?
Pacífico Bambu is an agri-industrial bamboo company in El Azulillo, Candelaria, Oaxaca, Mexico.
**Strategic vision:** grow premium Guadua angustifolia at scale, primary-process on-site (treated poles + slats), and become Mexico's first commercial exporter of structural Guadua to the US construction market via USMCA — targeting first US shipment in 2037.

**Ofir's role:** Managing Partner & Head of Growth — fundraising, investor relations, pitch deck, financial model, business strategy. Compensation: milestone-vested equity (up to 15%) + 5% cash success fee on capital raised.

## Strategic Thesis
Pacífico Bambu is **the first commercial exporter of structural Guadua bamboo from Mexico to the US construction market**. Not competing in the low-margin Mexican domestic market.

**Why first-mover holds:**
- Larger Mexican plantations exist (Tosepan 450 ha indigenous coop, Volkswagen 350 ha environmental compliance, Guadua Selecto 90 ha commercial domestic) — but **none export structural Guadua to the US**
- DataMéxico (HS 1401.10) confirms: 2024 Mexican bamboo exports = $0 USD
- US imports $23M/year of bamboo (2022) almost entirely from China & Vietnam — buyers seeking nearshore alternatives
- USMCA = 0% duty on bamboo (HS 1401.10) and processed slats (HS 4409.21)

**First export target: 2037** — only after 3 consecutive years of 50K+ poles proven (2034-2036). Until then: Mexican market + US niche builders (no spec needed).

## Key Risks & Barriers
1. **Biological gestation** — Guadua needs 5-7 years before full production. 2026 densification means new seedlings mature 2031+.
2. **Logistics** — transporting whole culms is expensive ("transporting air"). Primary processing on-site (slats) solves this.
3. **Certifications** — ICC-ES AC162 (12-24 mo, $80-200K) is the long-term moat. Funded from Round 2 earmark + operating cash flow 2034-2037.
4. **Minimum scale** — Experts cite 5,000 ha for international commodity export. Pacífico's strategy: niche premium (110 ha is enough for structural Guadua specialty market).
5. **Land title** — Juan's land regularization in progress.
