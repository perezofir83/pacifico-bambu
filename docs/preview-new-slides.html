Advanced Stress Test

Beyond Yield: 4 Adverse Scenarios

The sensitivity analysis shows profitability even at 1,000 poles/ha. But yield is only one variable. Here we model biological shock, demand softening, cashflow delay, and combined adverse case.

Scenario 1 · Biological Shock

Trigger: documented Guadua fungal disease (Bolaño-Garzón et al., SciELO Colombia) or extreme drought. Yield -50% year 5+ for 2 cycles.

Yield700 culms/ha
Revenue / 110 ha~26M (-51%)
Profit / 110 ha~21M (-55%)
Still profitableYES

Mitigation: 5-year R&D pilot validated 1,500 surviving plants (of 3,000) → robust phenotype. Genetic diversity + 3 species in pilot.

Scenario 2 · Demand Softening

Trigger: Lamboo/BamCore offtake fails to materialize; MX premium architecture market contracts. ASP drops 400 → 250 MXN. Yield unchanged.

Yield1,400 culms/ha
ASP250 MXN/pole
Revenue / 110 ha~32.7M (-37%)
Profit / 110 ha~26M (-44%)

Mitigation: the diversified channel model (slide 12) reabsorbs lower-grade product into stakes/biomass, avoiding single-channel saturation.

Scenario 3 · Cashflow Delay

Trigger: Round 2 (2028) delays 12 months; weather prevents 15 ha planting in 2027. Full maturity shifts from 2038 to 2040 (+2 years).

Full maturity2040 (was 2038)
Cum. revenue lost 2026-2040~80M MXN
Break-even2035 (was 2033)
40-yr ROI-12% vs base

Mitigation: PRONAFOR support (CONAFOR PFC: 30% on signing + 20% on planting prep + 50% post-survival) covers up to 50% of planting capex. Own land under 40-yr lease ensures continuity.

Scenario 4 · Combined Adverse (worst case)

Simultaneous triggers: yield -30%, ASP -25%, 1-year delay. Orthogonal stress on 3 dimensions.

Yield980 culms/ha
ASP300 MXN/pole
Revenue / 110 ha~25M (-52%)
Profit / 110 ha~20M (-57%)
Break-even2036 (vs 2033)

Result: still profitable (~$1.18M USD/yr net), payback shifts from 7 to 9 years post-investment. Thesis does not break.

Stress conclusion: even under 3 simultaneous stress dimensions, the model remains profitable. The thesis breaks only if simultaneously: (a) all 3 species in pilot die, (b) all 5 channels close, (c) Round 2 fails entirely. Joint probability < 1%.
